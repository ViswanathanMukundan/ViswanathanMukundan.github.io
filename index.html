About me

Currently an undergraduate in Information Technology at Sri Sairam Engineering College, Chennai.


My introduction to coding was purely out of necessity and not interest. When choosing streams after Class 10, I decided to go with Computer Science as the other options (Commerce and Biology) were too complicated. Programming started off with C++. And that got me hooked for life. What started off with basic programs like simple calculators, pattern printing, etc. has now evolved into a more challenging, and totally interesting domain with deep learning, neural networks, and more. I now feel that my approaching the subject with lots of enthusiasm in the early days and understanding the basics has now enabled me to seek out and approach complex problems on my own.
